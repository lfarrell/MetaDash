Search Digital New Zealand for a term or terms and view the metadata associated with your search. The metadata is shown as a bar chart, scatter plot and treemap.

The first chart shows the total number of items split out by metadata type. The second graph shows the distribution of your search term(s). Hover over a bar or circle to see the value returned. The final graph is a treemap which shows the metadata terms returned. Click the graph to expand a section. Once expanded you can click a term's box to search for it. Click outside the treemap to close the section.

The available metadata fields are: category, creator, language, provider, rights, subject and year. Metadata fields are based on which fields are facetable from the provider to reduce querying time.
