Search Europeana for a term or terms and view the metadata associated with your search. The metadata is shown as a bar chart, scatter plot and treemap.

Click the treemap to expand a section. Once expanded you can click a term's box to search for it. Click outside the treemap to close the section.

The available metadata fields are: country, provider, language, rights, type and year. Metadata fields are based on which fields are facetable from the provider to reduce querying time.
